About

Welcome to Endronex, a media outlet that focuses on drone racing coverage. Our aim is to bridge the missing link between racers and the public by; giving racers a platform to showcase their talents and providing a deeper insight into drones for the public.

Educate, Inspire, Create - this is what Endronex consists of and what we hope to pass on to others. Drone racing is a sport that encourages individuals to learn new skills, educate themselves in a variety of ways and develop a passion for technology. Through our Education and Innovation division, our Sector Heads lead fascinating debates on the implications of drones in reality whilst we invite professionals to provide discussions ranging from assembly tutorials to solving engineering problems.
